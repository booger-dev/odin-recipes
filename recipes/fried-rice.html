Fried Rice

Home

Fried rice is a simple and quick meal that is both delicious and filling. It doesn't require many ingredients and this recipe can easily be modfied with further ingredients of your choosing. In addition to broccoli, I often add a mixture of frozen mixed veggies to this mix for additional ruffage and nutrients. Experiment and find your perfect combination for this delicious fried rice recipe.

Ingredients

This recipe is for two bowls of fried rice. Scale the ingredients up or down to suit your needs.

  • Brown Rice (1/2 cup dry)
  • Eggs, 2 large
  • Chicken Breast, cubed, 6 oz
  • Broccoli, 8 oz

Steps

  1. Put brown rice in a pot with 2 cups of water and let boil until rice is soft.
  2. While rice is cooking, place chicken in a pan on high heat with olive oil and let brown.
  3. Once chicken has browned, pour broccoli into pan with chicken and cover until broccoli has become tender.
  4. Pour cooked rice into pan with chicken and broccoli.
  5. crack both eggs into rice mix and stir until eggs has cooked.
  6. Add seasonings of your choice and stir into rice mix.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
