Buffalo Chicken Burrito

Home

The buffalo chicken burrito is a zesty treat that is sure to please any crowd. Chicken. Rice. Peppers. Cheese. And hot buffalo sauce! What could be better to fill your tummy than this poppin' burrito.

Ingredients

This recipe is for two burrios. Scale the ingredients up or down to suit your needs.

  • Boneless Chicken Breast, 6oz (cubed)
  • Rice, 1/2 cup
  • Shredded Colby and Monterey Jack Cheese, 1/4 cup
  • Large Tortillas, 2
  • Green Bell Pepper, 1
  • Frank's Red Hot Buffalo Sauce

Steps

  1. In a pan coated with olive oil, let the chicken come to a nice brown.
  2. Add diced green bell peppers to chicken, and turn down the heat and cover for five minutes.
  3. Add chicken and bell peppers to center of each tortilla, covering with colby and monterey jack cheese.
  4. Add a sprinkle of Frank's Red Hot Buffalo Sauce to each burrito.
  5. Wrap and enjoy!
