Western Omelete

Home

A western omelette is a quick and delicious breakfast that doesn't require much to make. If your in a hurry, whip this up and enjoy a flavorful breakfast before you head off on your day.

Ingredients

This recipe is for one western omelette. Scale the ingredients up or down to suit your needs.

  • Eggs, 3 large
  • Onion, 1/8 cup, diced
  • Bell Peppers, 1/4 cup, diced
  • Tomato, 1/4 cup, diced
  • Salt, just a pinch
  • Black Pepper, just a pinch, ground

Steps

  1. Begin preparation by combining your onions, bell peppers and tomatoes into a single bowl.
  2. Crack all three eggs and stir until yolk is fully combined and pour into a skillet on medium heat.
  3. Stir until the eggs is about half cooked, then add a pinch of salt and pepper to the egg.
  4. Pour onion, bell pepper, tomatato mix into eggs and stir until all is evenly spread.
  5. Fold egg over half way and allow to cook for another minute.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
